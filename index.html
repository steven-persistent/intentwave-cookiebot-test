IntentWave + Cookiebot Test Page

This page is used to test the Cookiebot and IntentWave consent integration.

When you accept cookies, consent data should be sent to IntentWave automatically.

How to test:

  1. Accept cookies on the banner that appears
  2. Open browser DevTools → Console — you should see no errors
  3. Check your IntentWave dashboard for a new consent record
  4. Check your Cookiebot dashboard for consent activity
